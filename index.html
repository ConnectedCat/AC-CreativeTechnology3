Project 3: Synthetic relationships

Working with a partner create two electronic beings who are able to percieve some aspects of environment around them, communicate their findings and respond to the communications of their counterpart.

Things to consider: Are both creatures going to be in the same environment or in different ones? How will their sensory perceptions be processed internally? How will the result of this process be encoded for transmission? Is their communication going to be visible to the outsiders? Is it going to be one-way at a time or full duplex?

Documentation: write down your responses to the questions posed in the brief. Create a diagram outlining the communication schema and depicting all your decisions. Create a video explaning the inner workings of your system and showing it in action.
